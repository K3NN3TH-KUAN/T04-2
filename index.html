Understanding Energy Consumption in Australian Homes

Energy Star Ratings

Learn about Australia's mandatory Energy Rating Label system that helps consumers make informed decisions about appliance efficiency. The more stars, the more energy efficient the appliance.

Average Household Consumption

The average Australian household consumes approximately 6,000-7,000 kWh of electricity per year, with appliances accounting for about 30% of total energy use.

Cost Savings

Choosing energy-efficient appliances can save Australian households up to $500 per year on electricity bills, while reducing environmental impact.
